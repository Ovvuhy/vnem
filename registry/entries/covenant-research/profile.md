# Covenant Research

Covenant Research is a public research and benchmarking site for a proposed governance layer for autonomous AI agents, including a whitepaper, Harbor agent configurations, governance rules, and preliminary Terminal-Bench results.

## Best For

- Maintainers tracking governance and benchmark claims for autonomous coding agents

- Watch when evaluating governance patterns for autonomous agent runs, but require independent reproduction before recommendation.
